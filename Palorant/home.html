HI, WE ARE
PALORANT

DEFY THE LIMITS

Palorant is a free-to-play tactical first-person shooter developed by Giot Games. It blends elements from classic tactical shooters like Counter-Strike with hero-based gameplay similar to Overwatch. Players compete in 5v5 matches where one team attacks by planting a spike (bomb) while the other defends.

PHEONIX

Duelist

Pheonix is a fiery duelist from the UK who uses the power of flames to control the battlefield with style and confidence. Known for his brash personality and aggressive playstyle, Phoenix excels in taking fights head-on. His abilities allow him to blind enemies with curved flashes, heal himself with fire, and create flame walls that block vision and restore his health. With his ultimate, "Run It Back," Phoenix can dive into combat fearlessly, knowing he’ll return to life if taken down during its duration.
